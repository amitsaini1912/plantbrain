# 🏭 PlantBrain — Industrial Knowledge Intelligence

> A unified asset & operations "brain" that ingests a plant's scattered documents —
> engineering drawings, maintenance records, safety procedures, inspection reports —
> and makes their collective knowledge instantly queryable, cited, and actionable.

**ET AI Hackathon 2026 · Problem Statement 8**

## The problem
Asset-intensive plants run across 7–12 disconnected document systems. Professionals
spend ~35% of their time searching for information, and fragmentation drives an
estimated 18–22% of unplanned downtime. With ~25% of experienced engineers retiring
within a decade, undocumented knowledge is walking out the door.

## What it does
- **Knowledge Copilot** — ask questions across the whole corpus; get answers with citations and confidence.
- **Knowledge Graph** — auto-extracts equipment, procedures, and their relationships.
- **Maintenance & RCA assist** — fuses failure history and manuals for root-cause support.

## Tech stack
Python · Streamlit · ChromaDB · Google Gemini · PyMuPDF · NetworkX
